Since 1982

Where Silence Speaks volumes.

BlissHaven was born from a singular vision: to translate the architectural principles of light and space into the intimate objects of the home. We believe furniture is not merely functional, but the silent container of our lives.

The Materiality of Being

We source only the purest materials. Our Carrara marble is selected by eye from historic quarries, our wood is sustainably harvested European Oak, and our textiles are woven by a family-owned mill in Northern Italy.
